grafting

procedure:

  • Select scion and rootstock of compatible species and diameter
  • Prepare scion and rootstock by making diagonal cuts
  • Match the cuts of scion and rootstock to ensure proper alignment
  • Secure scion to rootstock using grafting tape or rubber bands
  • Apply grafting wax or sealant to protect the graft union
  • Monitor the graft for signs of successful union and growth
  • Remove any competing shoots or buds from the graft
  • Provide appropriate care and maintenance to promote graft success
  • Gradually remove support materials once graft union is established
  • Continue monitoring and caring for the grafted tree to ensure long-term health
