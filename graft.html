How to graft trees

steps:

  • Select the Rootstock and Scion
  • Prepare the Rootstock
  • Prepare the Scion
  • Secure the Graft
  • Protect the Graft
  • Monitor and Care
  • Gradual Transition

Instructions:

  1. Choose a healthy rootstock tree and a scion with desirable traits.
  2. Make a diagonal cut at the top of the rootstock.
  3. Cut the scion at a matching angle to fit onto the rootstock, ensuring it has at least one bud.
  4. Bind the two parts tightly using grafting tape or rubber bands.
  5. Apply grafting wax or sealing compound to the graft union.
  6. Keep the grafted tree in a protected environment with adequate moisture and sunlight.
  7. Gradually acclimate the grafted tree to its permanent location once healed.
